5.3. CRI Penalties and Malfeasance Strikes. You acknowledge that the CRI system applies penalties automatically based on published, deterministic rules. CRI is a composite score from 0 to 100 computed from 10 weighted components including transaction history, counterparty diversity, dispute record, and concentration penalties. The formula uses logarithmic scaling to prevent gaming through volume alone.

When an admin issues a malfeasance strike against a Node, the Node's CRI is recalculated with heavy strike penalty and balance may be confiscated to the Vault. Three malfeasance strikes result in permanent ban: CRI is set to 0, the Node is deactivated, and all remaining balance is confiscated.

Changes to CRI rules and slashing thresholds will be communicated via Grid announcement at least fifteen (15) days before taking effect. The rules applicable to any given trade are those in effect at the time the trade is submitted for settlement.
